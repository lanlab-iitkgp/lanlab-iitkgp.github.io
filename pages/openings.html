Openings

LAN Lab is growing, and we are keen to work with motivated researchers who enjoy hard, open-ended problems at the intersection of causal AI, reinforcement learning, game theory, and next-generation networks. Current opportunities are listed below.

PhD Positions

We are looking for motivated PhD candidates to join the lab's research on causal AI, reinforcement learning, game theory, and next-generation networks. A strong mathematical foundation is essential, particularly in probability, stochastic processes, optimization, and machine learning. Beyond technical preparation, we place real value on intellectual curiosity. The research here rewards candidates who enjoy open-ended mathematical problems and creative, non-standard thinking, rather than those who are most comfortable with routine textbook exercises.

We especially encourage applications from candidates with a GATE rank within the top 50, or with a Bachelor's degree from an IIT or NIT and a strong CGPA. Candidates with a top-50 GATE rank may also be eligible for the Institute's Director's Platinum Jubilee Excellence PhD Fellowship (DPEF); current details are available on the admission page linked below.

Admission is through IIT Kharagpur's round-the-year (rolling) PhD admission process, with applications reviewed each quarter. Prospective candidates should apply through the official portal, and are also welcome to write to me beforehand to discuss research directions and fit.

MS by Research

An MS by Research position in the lab is available through project funding. Candidates who join one of the lab's funded research projects, and who meet the institute's eligibility requirements based on their Bachelor's degree, can be considered for enrolment in the MS by Research programme. If this route interests you, please get in touch to discuss current project openings and eligibility.
