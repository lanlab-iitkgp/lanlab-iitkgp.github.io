People

The LAN Lab at GSSST, IIT Kharagpur, led by Dr. Dibbendu Roy, works at the intersection of causal AI, reinforcement learning, and 6G networks.

LAN Lab is led by Dibbendu Roy, Assistant Professor at the G.S. Sanyal School of Telecommunications, IIT Kharagpur.
